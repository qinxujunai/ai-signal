🎙️ 深度播客
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
Unsupervised Learning
💡 核心结论 · The Takeaway
LLM 不是通往人类级智能的入口；真正的智能需要能够预测行动后果并在此之上进行计划和搜索的世界模型。离开 Meta 创办 Ami，正是为了全力押注这条被主流暂时搁置的路。
LLMs are not a path to human-level intelligence; genuine intelligence requires world models that can predict the consequences of actions and plan via search and optimization. Leaving Meta to found Ami is LeCun’s full commitment to this neglected path.
🎯 如果你对‘Scaling LLM 是否是唯一道路’感到困惑，LeCun 这次给出了从认知科学、架构对比到离开 Meta 创业的全链路解释，适合所有在语言模型与真实世界 AI 间犹豫的技术决策者。
LLM 对语言极其有效，但现实世界是高维、连续、嘈杂的，比语言复杂得多，单纯生成像素的世界模型走不通。
LLMs are extraordinarily good at language manipulation, but the physical world is high-dimensional, continuous, and noisy; generating pixels is a dead end for world models.
"Reality is way more complicated than language because it's high dimensional. It's continuous. It's noisy. It's messy."
智能行为的两个核心能力：预测行动后果，以及通过搜索与优化进行规划。当前 LLM 的逐 token 预测完全不具备这两点。
The two essential characteristics of intelligent behavior are predicting the consequences of one’s actions and planning via search. Current token-by-token LLM inference has neither.
"LLMs do not have the ability to predict the consequences of their actions, and they do not have any planning abilities, because inference is by predicting the next token."
视觉语言动作模型（VLA）业已失败，不可靠且需海量训练数据。真正智能的系统不会逐动作自回归预测，而是通过内部世界模型搜索最优动作序列。
Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models are now widely seen as a failure, being unreliable and data-hungry. Real intelligence searches for an optimal sequence of actions using an internal world model, not autoregressive action prediction.
"VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. Like, it's really not working."
他离开 Meta 不是因为项目被砍，恰恰是因为项目被高层坚信，但全组织已完全聚焦 LLM 的追赶战，无法支持一条完全不同的架构路径；同时也发现主要应用（工业制造等）并非 Meta 的兴趣所在。
He left Meta not because his project was cancelled, but because the entire organization was refocused on catching up with LLMs, leaving no room for a fundamentally different architecture, and the key applications (e.g., manufacturing) were outside Meta's interest.
"Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore."
放弃像素级生成的世界模型是他五年前的‘顿悟’。人的思维认知运作在抽象表征层面，而非图像细节，这直接启发了他选择的架构方向。
His epiphany five years ago was that human mental models predict at an abstract level of representation, not at the pixel level. This insight drove his architectural choices.
"We can't predict this at a pixel level. And so our mental model of the world predicts, but at an abstract level of representation."
