AI 教父之一 Yann LeCun 在 Unsupervised Learning 播客中详细解释了他离开 Meta 创办新公司 Ami（Advanced Machine Intelligence）的原因。核心观点：LLM 对语言处理很好，但无法通往人类甚至动物级别的智能。Ami 专注于构建世界模型（world models），让 AI 系统能够预测自身行为的后果并进行规划——这是 LLM 所缺乏的关键能力。LeCun 认为 Meta 在 LLM 竞赛中过于专注，导致探索性研究被边缘化，而他需要一家初创公司来加速推进 JEPA 架构。
AI godfather Yann LeCun explains his departure from Meta to found Ami (Advanced Machine Intelligence) on the Unsupervised Learning podcast. His core thesis: LLMs are great for language manipulation but are not a path to human-level or even animal-level intelligence. Ami focuses on world models that enable AI systems to predict consequences of their actions and plan—capabilities LLMs fundamentally lack. LeCun felt Meta's increasing focus on catching up in the LLM race left exploratory research deprioritized, making a startup the right vehicle to scale the JEPA architecture.

🎙️ 深度播客
Unsupervised Learning
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
💡 核心结论
LLM 无法通往通用智能，因为它们缺乏两个关键能力：预测自身行为后果的能力，以及通过搜索进行规划的能力。世界模型（world models）才是正确的方向。
LLMs cannot lead to general intelligence because they lack two essential capabilities: predicting consequences of their own actions, and planning through search. World models are the correct path.
🎯 任何关注 AI 基础架构方向的人必听。LeCun 作为 AI 教父，详细解释了他为何认为整个行业在 LLM 上走错了方向，以及他押注的世界模型架构为何不同。
LLM 对语言处理很好，但语言是现实世界的简化版本。现实世界是高维、连续、嘈杂且混乱的，理解物理世界比理解语言难得多。
LLMs are great for language, but language is a simplified version of reality. The real world is high-dimensional, continuous, noisy, and messy—understanding it is much harder than understanding language.
"现实比语言复杂得多，因为它是高维的、连续的、嘈杂的、混乱的。训练一个系统理解现实世界要困难得多。"
Reality is way more complicated than language because it's high dimensional. It's continuous. It's noisy. It's messy. And training a system to understand the real world is much, much harder.
LLM 没有规划能力，因为它们通过预测下一个 token 进行推理，而不是通过搜索。智能行为需要两个关键特征：预测行为后果的能力，以及通过优化和搜索进行规划的能力。
LLMs have no planning ability because inference is by predicting the next token, not by search. Intelligent behavior requires two key characteristics: ability to predict consequences of actions, and ability to plan through optimization and search.
"LLM 没有预测自身行为后果的能力，也没有任何规划能力，因为推理是通过预测下一个 token 完成的，而不是通过搜索。"
LLMs do not have the ability to predict the consequences of their actions, and they do not have any planning abilities, because inference is by predicting the next token. Right? It's not by search.
VLA（视觉语言动作模型）已被视为失败方向——不够可靠、需要太多训练数据。世界模型才是正确的路径。
VLA (Vision-Language-Action models) is now seen as a failure—not reliable enough, requiring too much training data. World models are the correct path.
"VLA 现在显然被认为行不通。真的不起作用。"
VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. Like, it's really not working.
LeCun 离开 Meta 的原因是公司越来越专注于追赶 LLM 竞赛，探索性研究被边缘化。同时，Ami 的大多数应用场景（如制造业）与 Meta 的核心兴趣不符。
LeCun left Meta because the company became increasingly focused on catching up in the LLM race, deprioritizing exploratory research. Additionally, most applications of Ami's technology (like manufacturing) don't align with Meta's core interests.
"公司其他部门完全专注于 LLM，这让我清楚地意识到 Meta 不再是推动那个项目的合适地方了。"
The rest of the company was just totally entirely focused on LLMs and made it clear to me that Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore.
