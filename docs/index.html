Yann LeCun, AI godfather, founder of Ami Labs
Yann LeCun 离开 Meta 创办 Ami，押注世界模型架构 · Yann LeCun leaves Meta, founds Ami to bet on world model architecture
Yann LeCun 在最新播客中解释了他离开 Meta 创办 Ami Labs 的原因：Meta 已完全聚焦于 LLM，而他认为 LLM 无法通向人类级智能。Ami 的核心是 JEPA 架构，目标是构建能预测行动后果的‘世界模型’，而非生成像素。LeCun 强调，真正的智能需要两个能力：预测行动后果，以及通过搜索和优化进行规划——而这两点恰恰是当前 LLM 所缺乏的。
In a new podcast, Yann LeCun explains why he left Meta to found Ami Labs: Meta is now fully focused on LLMs, but he believes LLMs cannot lead to human-level intelligence. Ami's core is the JEPA architecture, aiming to build 'world models' that predict consequences of actions, not generate pixels. LeCun argues that true intelligence requires two capabilities: predicting consequences of actions, and planning via search and optimization—both of which current LLMs lack.

Unsupervised Learning
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
💡 核心结论 · The Takeaway
LLM 无法通向人类级智能，因为它们缺乏两个关键能力：预测行动后果的能力，以及通过搜索和优化进行规划的能力。世界模型（JEPA 架构）才是正确方向。
LLMs cannot lead to human-level intelligence because they lack two key capabilities: predicting consequences of actions, and planning via search and optimization. World models (JEPA architecture) are the right direction.
🎯 任何关注 AI 基础架构方向的人——尤其是对 LLM 局限性有疑虑、想了解世界模型替代方案的工程师和创始人——都应该听这一期。LeCun 用清晰的逻辑解释了为什么他认为 LLM 是死胡同，以及他押注的 JEPA 架构为何不同。
LLM 擅长语言操作，但现实世界远比语言复杂——高维、连续、嘈杂。训练系统理解现实世界要困难得多。
LLMs excel at language manipulation, but the real world is far more complex—high-dimensional, continuous, noisy. Training systems to understand the real world is much harder.
"Reality is way more complicated than language because it's high dimensional. It's continuous. It's noisy. It's messy. And training a system to understand the real world is much, much harder."
现实远比语言复杂，因为它是高维的、连续的、嘈杂的、混乱的。训练系统理解现实世界要困难得多。
世界模型的核心是预测行动后果的能力。没有这个能力，就无法构建真正的代理系统。
The core of a world model is the ability to predict consequences of actions. Without this, you cannot build a true agentic system.
"I cannot imagine how you can even think of building an agentic system without that system having the ability to predict the consequences of its actions. That's pretty essential."
我无法想象你如何能考虑构建一个代理系统，而该系统没有预测其行动后果的能力。这是非常基本的。
VLA（视觉语言动作模型）已被证明是失败的方向——不够可靠，需要太多训练数据。
VLA (vision-language-action models) have proven to be a failure—not reliable enough, requiring too much training data.
"VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. It's really not working."
VLA 现在显然被认为行不通。它真的不起作用。
Meta 已完全聚焦于 LLM，导致探索性研究被边缘化。这是 LeCun 离开的主要原因。
Meta has become entirely focused on LLMs, pushing exploratory research to the sidelines. This is the main reason LeCun left.
"The rest of the company was just totally entirely focused on LLMs and made it clear to me that Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore."
公司其他部门完全专注于 LLM，这让我清楚地意识到 Meta 不再是推动那个项目的合适地方。
