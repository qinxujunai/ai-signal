AI godfather Yann LeCun announced on the Unsupervised Learning podcast that he left Meta to found Ami (Advanced Machine Intelligence). His core argument: LLMs are useful but not a path to human-level or animal-like intelligence. Ami focuses on world models and the JEPA architecture, emphasizing systems that can predict consequences of their own actions and plan via search, rather than autoregressive token prediction. LeCun says Meta became too LLM-focused to continue this research.

🎙️ 深度播客
Unsupervised Learning
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
💡 核心结论 · The Takeaway
LLM 不是通往通用人工智能的路径，世界模型（能预测自身行为后果并通过搜索规划的系统）才是。
LLMs are not the path to AGI; world models—systems that can predict consequences of their actions and plan via search—are.
🎯 任何对 AI 未来方向有疑问的从业者都该听——这是 LeCun 离开 Meta 后首次系统阐述他的技术路线和行业判断。
LLM 擅长语言但无法理解物理世界，现实世界是高维、连续、嘈杂的，比语言复杂得多。
LLMs are good at language but cannot understand the physical world, which is high-dimensional, continuous, noisy, and far more complex than language.
"现实世界比语言复杂得多，因为它是高维的、连续的、嘈杂的、混乱的。"
Reality is way more complicated than language because it's high dimensional. It's continuous. It's noisy. It's messy.
世界模型的核心是预测自身行为的后果，这是构建 agent 系统不可或缺的能力。
The core of a world model is predicting consequences of one's own actions—an essential capability for any agentic system.
"我无法想象你如何能考虑构建一个 agent 系统，而该系统没有预测自身行为后果的能力。"
I cannot imagine how you can even think of building an agentic system without that system having the ability to predict the consequences of its actions.
LLM 推理是逐 token 预测，没有搜索和规划能力；真正的智能需要搜索最优行动序列。
LLM inference is token-by-token prediction without search or planning; true intelligence requires searching for optimal action sequences.
"LLM 没有预测自身行为后果的能力，也没有任何规划能力，因为推理是通过预测下一个 token 完成的，而不是通过搜索。"
LLMs do not have the ability to predict the consequences of their actions, and they do not have any planning abilities, because inference is by predicting the next token. It's not by search.
VLA（视觉语言行动模型）已被视为失败——不够可靠、需要太多训练数据。
VLA (Vision-Language-Action models) are now seen as a failure—not reliable enough, requiring too much training data.
"VLA 现在显然被认为行不通。它真的不 work。"
VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. Like, it's really not working.
LeCun 离开 Meta 是因为公司过度聚焦 LLM，不再优先探索性研究，而 Ami 的应用方向（工业制造等）也与 Meta 核心兴趣不符。
LeCun left Meta because the company became too LLM-focused, deprioritizing exploratory research, and Ami's applications (industrial manufacturing) didn't align with Meta's core interests.
"Meta 真的不再是推动那个项目的合适地方了。"
Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore.
