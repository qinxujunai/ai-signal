Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong

💡 核心结论 · The Takeaway
LLM 是强大的语言工具，但无法实现人类水平的智能；拥有世界模型（预测行动后果并规划）的系统才是通往通用智能的唯一路径。
LLMs are powerful for language, but cannot lead to human-level AI; only systems with world models that predict action outcomes and plan can pave the way to general intelligence.
🎯 如果你想知道 AI 的下一个大方向，LeCun 对 LLM 的根本性批判以及 JEPA 世界模型的蓝图，会彻底挑战你对当前范式的信仰。
LLM 不能理解物理世界，因为现实是高维、连续且混乱的，而语言是离散的简化抽象。因此 LLM 不是通往人类智能的道路。
LLMs can't understand the physical world because reality is high-dimensional, continuous, and messy, while language is a discrete simplification. LLMs are not a path to human-like AI.
"They're great for what they do. They're just not a path towards human level or human like intelligence."
智能行为的两个必要条件：能够预测自己行动的后果，以及通过搜索和优化来规划行动序列，而不是自回归生成 token。
Intelligent behavior requires two things: the ability to predict the consequences of one's actions, and planning through search and optimization — not autoregressive token prediction.
"LLMs do not have the ability to predict the consequences of their actions, and they do not have any planning abilities."
联合嵌入预测架构（JEPA）通过预测表征而非像素，在学习图像和视频的表征上远超生成模型；生成式预测是失败的方向。
Joint Embedding Predictive Architectures (JEPA) learn better image and video representations by predicting representations, not pixels. Generative pixel prediction has been a consistent failure in representation learning.
AMI Labs 的目标：一至一年半内展示能应用于机器人、工业过程、医疗的层次化世界模型；玩笑称五年后实现世界统治。
AMI Labs aims to demonstrate hierarchical world models for robotics, industrial control, and healthcare within 12-18 months, with a joke about ‘complete world domination’ in five years.
Tapestry 项目通过联邦学习构建全球开放 LLM，参与者只交换参数向量而非原始数据，以此保护文化主权，防止被加州或中国的模型洗脑。
The Tapestry project uses federated learning to build a global open LLM where contributors share only parameter vectors, not raw data, to preserve cultural sovereignty and avoid dominance by Californian or Chinese models.
"The contributors would contribute parameter vectors. … That's the cultural aspect of Tapestry."
