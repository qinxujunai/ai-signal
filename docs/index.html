Yann LeCun 在最新播客中系统阐述了他离开 Meta 创办 Ami 的技术逻辑。他认为 LLM 虽有用，但本质上是语言操纵工具，无法预测自身行动的后果，也无法通过搜索进行规划。他主张世界模型（world model）才是通往智能的关键——系统必须能预测行动后果并通过优化搜索来规划。同时他指出 VLA（视觉语言行动模型）已被视为失败方向，因为不够可靠且需要过多训练数据。
In a new podcast, Yann LeCun systematically explains the technical logic behind leaving Meta to found Ami. He argues that LLMs, while useful, are essentially language manipulation tools that cannot predict the consequences of their own actions or plan through search. He advocates for world models as the key to intelligence—systems must be able to predict action outcomes and plan via optimization search. He also notes that VLA (vision-language-action models) are now seen as a failure, being unreliable and requiring too much training data.

Therefore, one vendor sharing best practices across companies is more efficient than each company learning on its own, and customer learnings should feed back into the core product. From chat systems to agent systems, the FDE model becomes a core competency for deploying AI at scale.

Unsupervised Learning
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
💡 核心结论 · The Takeaway
LLM 是优秀的语言工具，但永远无法达到人类或动物级别的智能，因为缺乏预测行动后果和通过搜索规划的能力——世界模型才是通往通用智能的正确路径。
LLMs are great language tools but will never achieve human or animal-level intelligence because they lack the ability to predict consequences of actions and plan through search—world models are the correct path to general intelligence.
🎯 任何对 LLM 局限性、世界模型架构或 AI 研究商业化路径感兴趣的人必听。LeCun 给出了最清晰的技术路线图，解释了为什么他认为整个行业走错了方向。
LLM 无法预测自身行动的后果，也无法进行规划——推理只是预测下一个 token，而不是通过搜索优化
LLMs cannot predict the consequences of their own actions and cannot plan—inference is just predicting the next token, not optimizing through search
"LLMs do not have the ability to predict the consequences of their actions, and they do not have any planning abilities, because inference is by predicting the next token. Right? It's not by search."
LLM 没有预测自身行动后果的能力，也没有规划能力，因为推理是通过预测下一个 token 完成的，而不是通过搜索。
VLA（视觉语言行动模型）已被视为失败方向——不够可靠，需要过多训练数据
VLA (vision-language-action models) are now seen as a failure—not reliable enough, requiring too much training data
"VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. Like, it's really not working."
VLA 现在显然被认为走不通，它真的不 work。
世界模型的核心是让系统能预测行动后果，然后通��搜索和优化来规划行动序列
The core of world models is enabling systems to predict action consequences, then plan action sequences through search and optimization
"A world model, at a very general level, is something that allows an agentic system to anticipate the consequences of its own actions."
世界模型在最一般的层面上，是让 agent 系统能够预见自身行动后果的东西。
人类对世界的预测是在抽象表征层面，而非像素层面——这是 JEPA 架构与生成式世界模型的根本区别
Human world prediction operates at abstract representation levels, not pixel levels—this is the fundamental difference between JEPA and generative world models
"There's no way we can predict this at a pixel level. And so our mental model of the world predicts, but at an abstract level of representation."
我们不可能在像素级别预测这些，所以我们的心智模型是在抽象表征层面进行预测的。
Meta 因追赶 LLM 而削减探索性研究，导致 LeCun 决定离开——世界模型的应用更多在工业制造领域而非 Meta 核心业务
Meta's refocus on catching up with LLMs cut exploratory research, leading LeCun to leave—world model applications are more in industrial manufacturing than Meta's core business
"The rest of the company was just totally entirely focused on LLMs and made it clear to me that Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore."
公司其他人完全专注于 LLM，这让我清楚 Meta 不再是推动这个项目的合适地方。
