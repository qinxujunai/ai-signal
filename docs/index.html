AI godfather Yann LeCun explains in detail why he left Meta to start Ami. He argues that LLMs, while useful, cannot lead to human-level or animal-level intelligence because they lack the ability to predict consequences of actions and plan. Ami focuses on world models and the JEPA architecture, aiming to build AI for the physical world, not just language. LeCun notes that Meta's shift away from exploratory research in the LLM race drove his decision to spin out.

🎙️ 深度播客
Unsupervised Learning
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
💡 核心结论
LLM 缺乏预测行动后果和规划的能力，因此无法通向人类级智能；世界模型 + 搜索规划才是正确路径。
LLMs lack the ability to predict consequences of actions and plan, so they cannot lead to human-level intelligence; world models + search-based planning is the correct path.
🎯 任何质疑 LLM 是否通往 AGI 的人、关注世界模型架构的研究者、以及想了解 Yann LeCun 为何离开 Meta 的从业者都值得一听。
LLM 本身没有问题，它们对语言处理非常有用，但这不是通向人类级或动物级智能的路径。
LLMs are not wrong per se—they are great for language manipulation—but they are not a path towards human-level or animal-like intelligence.
"LLM 本身没有问题，它们很擅长做它们做的事。但它们不是通向人类级或动物级智能的路径。"
There's nothing wrong with LLMs... They're great for what they do. They're just not a path towards human level or human like intelligence or even animal like intelligence.
智能行为需要两个核心能力：预测行动后果的能力，以及通过搜索和优化进行规划的能力。LLM 两者都不具备。
Intelligent behavior requires two core abilities: predicting consequences of actions and planning via search and optimization. LLMs have neither.
"LLM 没有预测行动后果的能力，也没有规划能力，因为推理是通过预测下一个 token 完成的，而不是通过搜索。"
LLMs do not have the ability to predict the consequences of their actions, and they do not have any planning abilities, because inference is by predicting the next token. It's not by search.
世界模型的核心是预测行动后果，但应在抽象表征层面而非像素层面进行预测。人类也无法精确预测水杯会如何倒下。
World models predict consequences of actions, but at an abstract level of representation, not at the pixel level. Humans also cannot precisely predict how a water bottle will fall.
"我们对世界的心理模型进行预测，但是在抽象表征层面。"
Our mental model of the world predicts, but at an abstract level of representation.
VLA（视觉语言行动模型）已被视为失败，因为不够可靠且需要过多训练数据。
VLA (Vision Language Action models) are now seen as a failure—not reliable enough and requiring too much training data.
"VLA 显然已被视为没有前途。它真的行不通。"
VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. It's really not working.
Meta 在 LLM 竞赛中逐渐放弃探索性研究，这是 LeCun 决定离开并创办 Ami 的直接原因。
Meta's shift away from exploratory research in the LLM race was the direct reason LeCun decided to leave and start Ami.
"公司其他部门完全专注于 LLM，让我清楚地意识到 Meta 不再是推动那个项目的合适地方了。"
The rest of the company was just entirely focused on LLMs and made it clear to me that Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore.
