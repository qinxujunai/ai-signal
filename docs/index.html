Unsupervised Learning
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
💡 核心结论
LLM 不是通往人类级智能的路径，因为它们缺乏两个核心能力：预测行动后果的能力，以及通过搜索和优化进行规划的能力。JEPA 世界模型架构正是为此设计。
LLMs are not a path to human-level intelligence because they lack two core capabilities: the ability to predict consequences of actions, and the ability to plan through search and optimization. The JEPA world model architecture is designed for exactly this.
🎯 任何对 AI 未来方向有疑问的人——尤其是 LLM 怀疑论者和世界模型信徒——都应该听这一期。LeCun 给出了离开 Meta 创办 Ami 的完整逻辑，以及对 LLM 范式的根本性质疑。
LLM 没有规划能力，因为推理只是预测下一个 token，而非搜索
LLMs have no planning ability because inference is by predicting the next token, not by search
"LLM 没有预测行动后果的能力，也没有任何规划能力，因为推理是通过预测下一个 token 完成的，而不是通过搜索。"
LLMs do not have the ability to predict the consequences of their actions, and they do not have any planning abilities, because inference is by predicting the next token. Right? It's not by search.
世界模型的核心是预测行动后果，然后通过搜索规划行动序列
The core of a world model is predicting consequences of actions, then planning action sequences through search
"如果你有这种能力，你就可以规划一系列行动来完成一个任务……你通过规划、推理、搜索和优化的过程来完成。你不是像 VLA 那样自回归地一个接一个预测行动。"
If you have this ability, then you can plan a sequence of actions to accomplish a task... You do this by planning, reasoning, by a process of search and optimization. You don't do this by predicting o
VLA（视觉语言行动模型）已被视为失败：不够可靠、需要太多训练数据
VLA (Vision Language Action models) are now seen as a failure: not reliable enough, require too much training data
"VLA 现在显然被视为行不通。真的不起作用。"
VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. Like, it's really not working.
人类的世界模型在抽象层次预测，而非像素级——JEPA 正是这样设计
The human world model predicts at an abstract level, not at the pixel level—JEPA is designed this way
"我们对世界的心理模型进行预测，但是在抽象的表示层次上。"
Our mental model of the world predicts, but at an abstract level of representation.
LeCun 离开 Meta 的原因是公司完全聚焦 LLM，探索性研究不再被优先考虑
LeCun left Meta because the company became entirely focused on LLMs, exploratory research was deprioritized
"公司的其他部分完全专注于 LLM，这让我清楚 Meta 不再是推动那个项目的合适地方。"
The rest of the company was just totally entirely focused on LLMs and made it clear to me that Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore.
