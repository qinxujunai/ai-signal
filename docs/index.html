Yann LeCun 详解为何离开 Meta 并押注 JEPA 世界模型 · Yann LeCun on leaving Meta and betting on JEPA world models
AI 教父 Yann LeCun 在 Unsupervised Learning 播客中解释了他离开 Meta 创办 Ami 的原因：LLM 虽然有用，但无法通向人类或动物级别的智能。他正在推进 JEPA 架构，旨在构建能理解物理世界、预测行动后果的世界模型，而非仅仅处理语言。他认为 Meta 已完全聚焦 LLM，不再适合探索性研究。
AI godfather Yann LeCun explains on the Unsupervised Learning podcast why he left Meta to start Ami: LLMs are useful but not a path to human or animal-level intelligence. He is advancing the JEPA architecture to build world models that understand the physical world and predict consequences of actions, not just manipulate language. He says Meta became entirely LLM-focused and no longer supported exploratory research.

Aaron Levie 认为 AI 与软件本质不同：软件交付后相对稳定，而 AI 的能力和最佳实践持续演进，底层模型升级会改变工作流。因此，一个供应商跨公司共享最佳实践比每家公司自己摸索更高效。随着从聊天系统转向 Agent 系统，前向部署工程（FDE）将成为大规模部署 AI 的核心能力。

As we move from chat to agentic systems, forward-deployed engineering becomes a core competency for deploying AI at scale.

🎙️ 深度播客
Unsupervised Learning
Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong
💡 核心结论
LLM 擅长语言处理，但无法预测行动后果、无法规划、无法理解物理世界——这三个能力才是智能的本质。JEPA 架构通过抽象层次预测而非像素预测来解决这个问题。
LLMs excel at language but cannot predict consequences of actions, cannot plan, and cannot understand the physical world—these three capabilities are the essence of intelligence. The JEPA architecture addresses this by predicting at abstract representation levels rather than pixel level.
🎯 每个认真思考 AI 未来方向的从业者都应该听。LeCun 清晰地解释了为什么 LLM 不是通往人类智能的路径，以及他正在构建的替代方案。
LLM 不是通往人类或动物智能的路径
LLMs are not a path to human or animal-level intelligence
"LLM 在它们擅长的领域很棒，但它们不是通往人类级别或类似人类智能、甚至动物级别智能的路径。"
LLMs are great for what they do. They're just not a path towards human level or human like intelligence or even animal like intelligence.
世界模型的核心：预测行动后果 + 通过搜索规划
Core of world models: predict consequences of actions + plan via search
"我无法想象如何构建一个没有预测行动后果能力的 Agent 系统。"
I cannot imagine how you can even think of building an agentic system without that system having the ability to predict the consequences of its actions.
VLA（视觉语言行动模型）已被视为失败
VLA (Vision-Language-Action models) now seen as a failure
"VLA 现在显然被认为走不通，它真的不 work。"
VLA is clearly now being seen as not going anywhere. It's really not working.
人类智能在抽象层次预测，而非像素层次
Human intelligence predicts at abstract levels, not pixel level
"我们对世界的心理模型进行预测，但是在抽象的表示层次上。"
Our mental model of the world predicts, but at an abstract level of representation.
Meta 已完全聚焦 LLM，不再适合探索性研究
Meta became entirely LLM-focused, no longer suitable for exploratory research
"公司其他部门完全聚焦于 LLM，这让我清楚 Meta 不再是推动那个项目的合适地方。"
The rest of the company was just entirely focused on LLMs and made it clear to me that Meta was really not the right place to push for that project anymore.
